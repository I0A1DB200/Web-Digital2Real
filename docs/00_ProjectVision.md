# Digital2Real — Project Vision

**Status:** Active
**Architecture authority:** `RFC-001_RepositoryArchitecture.md`

## Vision

Digital2Real is an editorial and educational platform for Industrial Automation. It transforms real engineering problems into practical knowledge, structured learning, and reusable technical experiences.

## Current Product Baseline

The active frontend is Notes-first:

- Engineering Notes is the default experience.
- Academy is an informational product view in development.
- About communicates the platform purpose.

Laboratory data and components remain preserved but are not connected to the active application. Their product disposition is pending.

## Platform Direction

Digital2Real is expected to support distinct product areas over time:

- Editorial Website;
- Engineering Notes;
- Academy;
- Digital2Real Assistant;
- shared reusable engines.

This direction does not imply that every product is currently implemented.

## Principles

- Engineering first
- Quality over quantity
- Learn by building
- Data-driven content
- Reusable capabilities
- Vendor neutrality
- Editorial design
- Evolution through verified requirements

The permanent governing principles are defined in `Constitution.md`.
